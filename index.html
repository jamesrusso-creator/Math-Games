Place-value die faces: 1/10, 1/100, 1/100, 1/1000, 1/1000, 1/1000.

How It Works

  • Roll the number die and the place-value die.
  • Single-click blocks to shade or unshade the exact decimal amount shown.
  • Double-click a tenth or hundredth block to break it into smaller place-value parts.
  • Use Check Result to confirm your choice and record the round.
  • If a roll is larger than the remaining unshaded amount, it counts as a missed turn.

How to Play

  1. Roll the dice to get a fraction
  2. Click on the fraction wall to shade in that fraction
  3. Try to fill rows completely without going over
  4. Record your rolls in the table
  5. The game ends when you can't fit the rolled fraction
