Learn fractions, decimals, and place value through interactive, engaging games.

Colour in Fractions

Roll the dice and shade fractions on the fraction wall. Build understanding of equivalent fractions and fraction addition.

Place That Number

Roll two digit dice, choose the strongest two-digit number, and place it on a 0 to 100 number line using benchmarks.

What You'll Learn

  • Visualize fractions and their equivalents
  • Understand decimal place value
  • Estimate positions on a number line
  • Practice addition with fractions and decimals
  • Build mathematical intuition through play

How to Play

  1. Roll the dice to get a fraction
  2. Click on the fraction wall to shade in that fraction
  3. Try to fill rows completely without going over
  4. Record your rolls in the table
  5. The game ends when you can't fit the rolled fraction
